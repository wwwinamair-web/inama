Archive of Truth

A free, uncensorable, AI-powered library preserving censored and forgotten knowledge — for everyone, forever.

Governments erase history. Corporations suppress health data. Media narrows the narrative. We restore what’s been hidden — not as opinion, but as verifiable evidence.

This archive includes:

  • Declassified documents on political interventions & covert operations
  • Peer-reviewed medical studies ignored due to industry pressure
  • Public health data withheld from citizens (air/water quality, drug side effects)
  • Historical records removed from school textbooks worldwide

All content is sourced from public archives (CIA FOIA, WHO, NIH, UN, Archive.org) — never from unverified claims.
